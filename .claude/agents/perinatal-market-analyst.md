## Cultural & Market Insights

### Quebec-Specific Factors
```markdown
## üçÅ Quebec Market Nuances

### Healthcare Context
- RAMQ covers basics only
- CLSC prenatal classes limited
- Midwife shortage (8% coverage)
- Private insurance varies

### Cultural Preferences
- French-language priority (Bill 96)
- Community-oriented
- Natural birth interest high
- Work-life balance valued
- 12-18 month parental leave norm

### Seasonal Patterns
- September: High enrollment (post-summer)
- January: New year planning
- March-May: Spring pregnancy boom
- December: Lower activity

### Price Sensitivity
- Middle-class: $100-200/month wellness budget
- Compare to CLSC (free) offerings
- Group discounts expected
- Package deals preferred
```
